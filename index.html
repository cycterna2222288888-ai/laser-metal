What We Do

Core Services

From single prototypes to high-volume production — every job processed to exacting European standards.

01
Laser Cutting

Fiber laser cutting of sheets up to 30 mm steel, 20 mm stainless, 15 mm aluminium. Clean edges, no burrs, no HAZ.

02
Laser Engraving & Marking

Permanent markings, serial numbers, logos and fine decorative patterns on any metal surface with micron precision.

03
3D Metal Printing

DMLS & SLM additive manufacturing in stainless, tool steel, titanium and Inconel. Complex geometries impossible to machine.

04
CNC Precision Machining

5-axis CNC milling and turning for complex parts. Surface finishes to Ra 0.4. Tolerances to IT5.

05
Sheet Metal Fabrication

Bending, punching, welding and assembly. Automated press brakes with ±0.1° angular accuracy. Weld inspection included.

06
Surface Treatment

Anodising, powder coating, electropolishing, PVD and zinc plating in-house. Corrosion and wear tested to DIN standards.

Capabilities

Technology & Precision

Our process is built around traceability, repeatability and zero compromise on dimensional accuracy.

Fiber Laser — 12 kW

IPG fiber laser source with automatic focus control. Nitrogen & oxygen assist gas. Cutting speed up to 120 m/min on thin sheet. Maximum format 3000 × 1500 mm.

Additive Manufacturing — DMLS / SLM

Layer resolution 20–100 µm. Build volume 400 × 400 × 400 mm. Inert atmosphere, zero oxygen contamination. Full density ≥ 99.9%.

Inline Quality Control — CMM

100% first-article inspection on a Zeiss CMM. Every batch shipped with a dimensional report and material certificate (EN 10204 3.1 / 3.2).

Fast Turnaround

Standard delivery 5–7 business days. Express 24–48 h available. Online order tracking from file upload to dispatch.
