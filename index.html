What We Do

Core Services

From single prototypes to high-volume production runs — every job processed to exacting European standards.

01
Laser Cutting

Fiber laser cutting of sheets up to 30 mm steel, 20 mm stainless, 15 mm aluminium. Clean edges, no burrs, no heat-affected zone.

02
Laser Engraving & Marking

Permanent serial numbers, logos and patterns on any metal surface. Micron precision, compliant with traceability standards.

03
3D Metal Printing

DMLS & SLM additive manufacturing in stainless, tool steel, titanium and Inconel. Complex geometries impossible to machine.

04
CNC Precision Machining

5-axis CNC milling and turning for complex parts. Surface finishes to Ra 0.4. Tolerances to IT5 grade.

05
Sheet Metal Fabrication

Bending, punching, welding and assembly. Automated press brakes with ±0.1° angular accuracy. Weld inspection included.

06
Surface Treatment

Anodising, powder coating, electropolishing, PVD and zinc plating in-house. Corrosion tested to DIN standards.

Technology

Built for Accuracy

Our process is built around traceability, repeatability and zero compromise on dimensional accuracy. Every part is inspected before it leaves the facility.

12 kW Fiber Laser — IPG source

Auto-focus control, nitrogen & oxygen assist. Cutting speed up to 120 m/min. Format up to 3000 × 1500 mm.

DMLS / SLM Additive Manufacturing

Layer resolution 20–100 µm. Build volume 400 × 400 × 400 mm. Full density ≥ 99.9%. Inert atmosphere.

Zeiss CMM — 100% First-Article Inspection

Every batch shipped with a dimensional report and material certificate EN 10204 3.1 / 3.2.

5–7 Day Standard · 24–48 h Express

Online order tracking from file upload to dispatch. Dedicated account manager on every order.
